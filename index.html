Victorian National Parks

Victoria is home to lots of natural wonders to explore. National Parks cover 2.8 Million Hectares or ~12% of Victoria's total land and helps make Victoria an amazing place to live and visit.

Visitor Insights

Since the 1960s multiple administrations have sought to establish new parklands to protect the diverse nature within our backyards. In 2002 the Victorian Government first recognised Marine National Parks adding new protections to maintain and preserve the Victoria's beauty.

National Park Visits have been growing exponentially. Over the past twenty years visit numbers have almost doubled from 24.9 Million visits in 2002 to 49.6 in 2018. From the pie chart most come for the walks with all forms accounting for ~45% of visits.
