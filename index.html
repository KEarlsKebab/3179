Victorian National Parks

Victoria is home to lots of natural wonders to explore. But only half of Victoria's national Parks have recognised and maintained campgrounds.

Growing Parks

In the 1960s with a growing urban sprawl, the Victorian Government rushed to protect its most valueable land for future generations to enjoy. This focus would stay around until the late 2000s. Regional Haven
